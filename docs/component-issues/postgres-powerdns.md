### High
#### secrets-handling
- Split app vs superuser credentials so they do not share the same Vault password property; use distinct Vault paths/properties and rotate independently. (ids: `dk.ca.finding.v1:postgres-powerdns:72d5b84dbdfc7496b621fb0ebb3e909226f0864c031a3b715c82f87a232ab333`; evidence: `platform/gitops/components/data/postgres/powerdns/base/externalsecret-app.yaml`, `platform/gitops/components/data/postgres/powerdns/base/externalsecret-superuser.yaml`, `platform/gitops/components/data/postgres/powerdns/base/patch-cluster.yaml`)
